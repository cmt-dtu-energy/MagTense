Basic Geometries¶

The basic tile geometries supported in MagTense are shown below.

Prism¶

The prism is defined by the side lengths \(a, b\) and \(c\) and with a center coordinate \(x_{offset}, y_{offset}\) and \(z_{offset}\).

An example of how to compute the magnetic field from a prism is given in matlab/examples/Validation_prism.

Cylindrical Tile¶

An example of how to compute the magnetic field from a prism is given in matlab/examples/Validation_cylindrical_slice.

Circpiece¶

An example of how to compute the magnetic field from a prism is given in matlab/examples/Validation_cylindrical_slice.

Inverted Circpiece¶

An example of how to compute the magnetic field from a prism is given in matlab/examples/Validation_cylindrical_slice.

Tetrahedron¶

An example of how to compute the magnetic field from a prism is given in matlab/examples/Validation_tetrahedron.
