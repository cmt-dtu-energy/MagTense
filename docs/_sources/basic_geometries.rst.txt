Basic Geometries
========================================
The basic tile geometries supported in MagTense are shown below.

========================================
Prism
========================================
.. image:: ./fig/prism.png
The prism is defined by the side lengths :math:`a, b` and :math:`c` and with a center coordinate :math:`x_{offset}, y_{offset}` and :math:`z_{offset}`.

An example of how to compute the magnetic field from a prism is given in matlab/examples/Validation_prism.

========================================
